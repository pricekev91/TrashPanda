# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is primarily a solo project, but contributions are welcome. Anyone who participates — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or feedback — agrees to interact respectfully and constructively.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Provide constructive feedback; critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification before assuming malice or incompetence.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its goals.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions.
- Publishing private information about others without their consent.

## Enforcement

This project is maintained by a single person. Reports of unacceptable behaviour can be made by opening a private issue or reaching out directly via GitHub. The maintainer reserves the right to remove contributions or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

---

_This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, and simplified for a lightweight, solo-friendly project._
